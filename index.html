SunE

What's SunE you ask?
Let us tell you about it !

Combined electronics with UV photosensitive materials, allow to provide an instant measurement of UV radiation to take action and protect your skin.

More than just a design

The eggshell UV photosensitive, starch/cellulose compound case, is extremeley strong, eco-friendly and changes color INSTANTLY when exposed to UV light. Once the UV sensor is faced to the sun, the multicolor LED will indicate the levels of risk for your skin. A mini brain is processing the information given by the sensor, providing exact information of radiation levels.

Sounds interesting,
but how do I use it?

SunE will tell you when to use it. By changing to color to a vivid purple or pink, it suggests you to check the UV radiation levels. To do so, just put it on your hand. Green, indicates low UV radiation levels. Yellow, medium. Red... high.

Why should I use it?

Ozone is certainly damaged and skin cancer is a growing issue. This tiny gadget will help you prevent absorbing those UVA and UVB rays that are extremely dangerous for the body. It will also let you know when your sunscreen protection is not working anymore and you need a second round.
